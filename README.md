# Superstore-Sales-dashboard
This is my first Power BI Sales Dashboard analyzing 38k orders with revenue worth $2.3M with actionable  business insights.
## project overview 
Intrective 3 page power bi dashbpard analyzing store performance over the year of 2014 to 2018 accross 38k orders worth $2.3M revenue.

## Business problem
Retail management needed data driven insights to identify profitable products across the categories , and to understand discount impacts on revenue and to optimize regional performance.

 ## Key Insights Discovered
 - Technology category derives the 50%+ to tal profits.
 - Furniture generates $18k losses dispite high sales.
 - Discount above 20% hurting profitability from both side lower sales and lower margins.
 - copiers and phone are top profit drivers.
